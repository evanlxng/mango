Foldable, Hybrid-Electric Skateboard (March-May 2021)

- Designed from start to finish, a folding personal electric vehicle using Fusion360 and hand-tools.
- Mounted a 3D printed battery housing to a skateboard deck, and attached 2, 350 watt electric hub motors to power the skateboard.
- Iteratively tested and modeled the battery housing and lid in CAD before printing to minimize waste and optimize on cost.
- In the 3D printing process, to optimize on time and for safety precautions , I set up OctoPi on my 3d printer.
- OctoPi is a software that runs on a Raspberry Pi unit that allows me to remotely control my 3D printer from anywhere on my home wifi to stop and start prints.
I can also monitor prints in real-time and ensure that I fix printing errors before they cause damage to my printer or spark a fire hazard.
