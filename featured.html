NASA and Texas Instruments Coding Contest (May-Aug 2020)

During the summer of 2020, I participated in the NASA and TI Coding Contest with a classmate.
Over the course of 5 rounds of competition, our presentation and project placed 3rd in the nation!
Together, we built a 3-stage water santization system with UV-C spectrum light to combat bacteria on the International Space Station

Below, you'll find a little portfolio presentation that showcases some of the highlights of our project and creation process.

Eagle Scout Project (May-June 2019)

Drafted and designed in SketchUp and Fusion360 CAD a system of 3 garden beds for a student garden at Waite H.S in Toledo.
Presented project ideas to a BSA committee and met with school administration to manage approval for the project.
Self-Fundraised $200+ for raw materials for the project, and led a group of Scouts to build and put the garden beds in place.
